# Privacy Policy — Automatic Tab Sorting

**Last updated:** 2025-04-15

## Data Collection

Automatic Tab Sorting does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data.

## Permissions

The extension requires the `tabs` permission solely to detect tab activation and reorder tabs within the browser window. No tab content, URLs, or browsing history is accessed or recorded.

## Third Parties

No data is sent to any third party. The extension operates entirely locally within the browser.
